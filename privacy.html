Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

SE Time is a work management and time tracking application used by organizations. Login is required to use the application.

SE Time is currently available in Norwegian only. This privacy policy is provided in English for clarity and app store review purposes.

1. Information we collect

We collect the information needed to provide secure access and time tracking functionality.

  • Name and email address
  • Login credentials managed through secure authentication
  • Organization membership and role information
  • Projects, tasks and time registrations
  • Device and login information used for security and authentication

2. How we use information

  • To authenticate users
  • To manage access to organizations and projects
  • To store and display work time registrations
  • To provide administrative and reporting features
  • To maintain security and reliability of the application

3. Data storage and security

Data is stored securely using cloud services hosted on Microsoft Azure. Access to data is restricted by authentication, user roles and organization-based data separation.

Communication between the app and backend services is protected using HTTPS.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is only accessible to authorized users within the relevant organization and to platform administrators when required for support, maintenance or administration.

5. Account access and deletion

Users may request correction or deletion of their account data by contacting their organization administrator or support. Some records may be retained where required for operational, legal or audit purposes.

6. Children’s privacy

SE Time is not intended for children and is intended for use by organizations and authorized users.

7. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
